## üìä Key Insights
The analysis revealed critical areas for intervention:

1.  **Profitability is Highly Uneven:** The map clearly shows that states in the Central/East regions (like **Texas, Illinois, and Pennsylvania**) are primary centers for losses, substantially dragging down overall company profit.
2.  **Stagnant Profit Despite Sales Growth:** The trend chart shows that while **Sales** volume is consistently increasing, total **Profit** has remained flat over the four-year period. This indicates rising costs or poor pricing strategies are neutralizing revenue growth.
3.  **High-Loss Products:** Specific Sub-Categories, such as **Tables** and **Bookcases**, demonstrate negative profit ratios, meaning the cost of fulfillment is not covered by the revenue generated.

## üìà Business Recommendations
Based on the data, the following two strategies are recommended:

1.  **Implement Geo-Targeted Loss Mitigation:** Review logistics and adjust pricing or shipping rules for the top three loss-incurring states to neutralize negative profit contributions.
2.  **Execute Product Portfolio Review:** Launch an immediate cost analysis on high-loss items like Tables. The company must either increase their price, find a cheaper supplier, or discontinue/replace them.
